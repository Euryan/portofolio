My Story

My journey into technology began when I was introduced to Arduino in junior high school. What started as curiosity quickly transformed into a deep passion for building things that solve real-world problems.

Since then, I've dedicated myself to learning robotics, IoT systems, and machine learning. Each project has taught me something new, from the importance of clean code to the value of collaborative problem-solving.

Beyond technical skills, I've developed a strong interest in ethical technology. I believe that as creators, we have a responsibility to consider the impact of what we build on society and the environment.

Today, I continue to explore new technologies while maintaining a commitment to creating meaningful, responsible innovations. My goal is to use technology as a tool for positive change.
